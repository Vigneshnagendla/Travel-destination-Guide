New Digha Beach

Newly-Developed Beach


Overview

New Digha Beach, situated in the coastal town of Digha in West Bengal, India, is a popular tourist destination known for its pristine sandy shores and scenic beauty.


Location

New Digha Beach is located in the northeastern part of Digha town, overlooking the Bay of Bengal.


What To Expect?

You can go for long walks, soak in the glorious sunrise and sunset views, and take a dip in the foamy waters of the Bay of Bengal.


Suggested Duration- 1-2 hours

Tips

  • Follow all the safety instructions before taking a plunge into the sea.
  • Don’t forget to catch the sunrise here.
  • Don't hesitate to bargain when shopping at local markets or hiring services.
  • Do not venture into the sea before consulting the lifeguard on duty, especially during the monsoons.
  • Before stepping out in the sun, don’t forget to apply sunscreen on the exposed areas of your skin.
