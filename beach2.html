Pondicherry

Paradise Beach

Paradise Beach Paradise Beach in Pondicherry, India, is a popular destination for both locals and tourists visiting the region. It's also known locally as Plage Paradiso.

Tranquebar, Tamil Nadu

Tranquebar, Tamil Nadu Placed at the confluence of the Kaveri river and the Bay of Bengal, Tranquebar is decorated with swaying palms and rugged houses overlooking glittery waves of the ocean.

Serenity Beach

Serenity Beach is a beautiful and serene coastal stretch located near the town of Pondicherry in the Tamil Nadu. It's one of the more popular beaches in the area, known for its golden sands, and clear blue waters.
