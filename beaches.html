Famous Beaches in India

Pondicherry

Pondicherry Beach , also known as Promenade Beach, is a popular stretch of coastline in the heart of Pondicherry town.

Goa

Goa, a coastal state in western India, is renowned for its vibrant nightlife, sandy beaches, and Portuguese colonial architecture.

Varkala

Varkalais a captivating coastal town nestled along the Arabian Sea in the Indian state of Kerala.


Digha

Digha Nestled on the coast of the Bay of Bengal, Digha is about 4.5 hours from Kolkata. It is known for its laidback beaches, picturesque vistas and diverse marine life.

Kovalam

KovalamDotted with swaying palms and fondly known as the 'Paradise of the South', Kovalam is a tranquil seaside town nestled along the picturesque coastlines of the Arabian Sea.

Gokarna

GokarnaGokarna is a serene coastal town located in the Uttara Kannada district of Karnataka, India. Gokarna offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and spiritual charm.
